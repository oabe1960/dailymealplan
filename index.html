Breakfast: Akara and Pap

Ingredients:
  • Black-eyed peas
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying
Preparation:
  1. Soak black-eyed peas overnight and peel.
  2. Blend with onions and pepper until smooth.
  3. Season with salt and deep-fry spoonfuls in hot oil until golden brown.
Sourcing:

Available at local markets or grocery stores.

Lunch: Jollof Rice with Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Chicken
  • Spices
Preparation:
  1. Cook blended tomatoes, onions, and peppers in oil.
  2. Add rice and water, cook until rice is tender.
  3. Season and serve with grilled chicken.
Sourcing:

Available at supermarkets or local farmers' markets.

Dinner: Efo Riro (Vegetable Soup) with Pounded Yam

Ingredients:
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Meat (beef or chicken)
  • Pounded yam
Preparation:
  1. Cook meat and blend tomatoes, onions, and peppers.
  2. Add the blended mix to the meat and simmer.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Serve with pounded yam.
Sourcing:

Available at African grocery stores or local markets.
